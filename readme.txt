This plugin adds info of your Grooveshark to your widgetizable sidebar.

== Description ==

This plugin get the info of your account in Grooveshark, and adds it to the sidebar of your blog for share it with your audience.



== Installation ==

So simple! Uncompress the tar.gz that you downloaded, and upload the files to your wp-content/plugins/ folder.
Thats all, folks!
